Hiring Process

Wanna work for one of our companies
Well we are always looking for hardworking talented people to help us change then world, here is sneek peak of the hiring process, have a look.

Resume

We hire through a nummber of ways either you will directly get contacted by us through an agency or you can yourself mail us your resume.

Selection

If your resume get selected by our HR team you will recive a mail from us with a selection letter, one of our Hrs will get in touch with you personally and will take a telephonic interview.

Inbound/Outbound

We can select you to work in any of our companies around the globe, so if you got selected for an overseas position we will contact our agency in your country to take over the process of verification.

All Done

If the Verification, medical and documents process all goes well, then we will issue you a working visa and you are onboard.
